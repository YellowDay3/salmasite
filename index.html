About Trees

Trees are vital to the health of our planet. They provide oxygen, improve air quality, and support ecosystems. Learn more about the different types of trees and how they impact the environment.

Why Trees Matter

  • Air Purification: Trees absorb carbon dioxide and release oxygen.
  • Climate Control: Trees help cool the environment and mitigate the urban heat island effect.
  • Biodiversity: Trees provide shelter for various species of animals and insects.
  • Soil Conservation: Their roots prevent erosion and improve soil quality.

How to Plant a Tree

Planting a tree is a rewarding way to contribute to environmental sustainability. Follow these steps for success:

  1. Choose the right type of tree for your climate and location.
  2. Dig a hole twice the width of the root ball.
  3. Place the tree in the hole and fill it with soil, ensuring the tree is straight.
  4. Water the tree regularly and mulch around the base to conserve moisture.
  5. Maintain your tree as it grows by pruning and checking for pests.
